About Us

The History of Pigraid

Pigraid is a Minecraft server known for its engaging community and unique gameplay experiences. Founded in the early 2010s, it began as a small server focusing on survival and creative modes. Over time, it evolved to incorporate various game modes, including mini-games, PvP, and role-playing elements.

The server gained popularity due to its active moderation, regular updates, and community events. Players appreciated the emphasis on player interaction and creativity, leading to a dedicated fan base. Pigraid often hosts seasonal events and builds contests, further enhancing the community spirit.

As Minecraft itself evolved, Pigraid adapted, implementing new features and plugins to keep gameplay fresh. Today, it stands out for its inclusive environment and a diverse range of activities, making it a beloved destination for Minecraft players.

The New PigRaid Server

The new PigRaid server aims to honor its rich history while embracing modern Minecraft standards. It will incorporate classic elements that long-time players loved, such as survival gameplay and creative building challenges, while introducing updated features that reflect current community trends.

Key enhancements include:

  • Diverse Game Modes: Expanding beyond survival and creative to include mini-games, PvP arenas, and role-playing environments.
  • Custom Plugins: Implementing popular plugins for enhanced gameplay, such as economy systems, land claiming, and unique crafting options.
  • Community Events: Regularly scheduled events like build contests, seasonal celebrations, and tournaments to foster community engagement.
  • Quality of Life Improvements: Enhanced server performance, anti-grief measures, and user-friendly interfaces for a smoother experience.
  • Active Moderation: A dedicated team to ensure a positive environment, helping new players integrate while maintaining community standards.

By combining nostalgic features with innovative updates, the new PigRaid server seeks to create a vibrant, welcoming space for both veteran players and newcomers alike.

Our Community

Without you, none of this would be possible. We are so grateful for the dedication and support you've shown throughout the years, and we look forward to continuing this journey together.
